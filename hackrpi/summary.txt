question: what is the status of the sudanese civil war in november 2025?
topic: sudanese civil war
summary: as of november 2025, the sudanese civil war continues with ongoing conflicts between the sudanese armed forces (saf) and the rapid support forces (rsf). the rsf, led by general mohamed hamdan dagalo, controls most of darfur and parts of khartoum. the saf, led by general abdel fattah al-burhan, has regained some territory, including the presidential palace and airport in khartoum by march 2025. the humanitarian crisis remains severe, with widespread famine affecting millions, and no lasting ceasefire has been reached.
